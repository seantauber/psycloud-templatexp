Instructions

In this study we are asking you to make judgments about the cause of some events. You will be given the outcomes of some events and some background information, and then asked to make judgments about the cause of the events.

For each event, you will use a slider to judge the probability that it has a particular cause. Your answers can range from 0% probablity (all the way to the left) to 100% probability (all the way to the right). All of the sliders are initially set to 0%.
